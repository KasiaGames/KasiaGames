Once upon a time, there was a curious little girl named Addie who loved to explore the woods around her house. One day, while wandering deep into the forest, she stumbled upon an old abandoned cabin. As she cautiously approached the door, she noticed there was a faint light flickering inside.

Addie was astounded and immediately jumped off the dragon's back to search for the gemstone. After minutes of searching, she finally found the stone, clutched it in her hand, closed her eyes, and made her wish.

So they flew and flew over the trees until they reached an enchanted waterfall. The dragon told Addie that there was a magical gemstone hidden behind the falls and that whoever found it could make any wish they desired.

From that day on, Addie visited the dragon and the enchanted waterfall frequently, wishing for different things every time. She never forgot how much magic exists in the world and was forever grateful for her dragon friend and the adventures they shared.

When she opened her eyes, she found herself back in her bed, a little disorientated and confused. But then she realized that her room had transformed into a beautiful castle, with all of her favorite things and toys strewn about. Her wish had come true!
